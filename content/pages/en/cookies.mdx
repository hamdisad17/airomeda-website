# Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies airomeda.com uses and why.

## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed in your browser when you visit a website. Cookies help a site remember information — such as your language preference or whether you have completed a security check — so the site works correctly across page visits.

## Cookies we use

We use **only strictly necessary and functional** cookies. We do not set any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

| Cookie Name | Category | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|----------|---------|----------|
| `NEXT_LOCALE` | Strictly necessary / Functional | Stores your language preference (English or Turkish) so the correct language is served on each visit. | 1 year |
| `cf_clearance` | Strictly necessary / Security | Set by Cloudflare Turnstile. Confirms that a form submission passed the bot-protection challenge. | 30 days |
| `__cf_bm` | Strictly necessary / Security | Cloudflare bot-management cookie. Distinguishes legitimate visitors from automated bots. | Session (~30 minutes) |

> **Note:** Cloudflare Turnstile cookies are only active on pages that contain a form (Contact, Demo Request, Career Application). They are not set on content-only pages.

### About Cloudflare Turnstile

Forms on our site use Cloudflare Turnstile to protect against spam and bot submissions. This service is operated by Cloudflare, Inc. (United States). When a form loads, technical data such as your browser fingerprint and IP address may be transmitted to Cloudflare. For Cloudflare's privacy policy see [cloudflare.com/privacypolicy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Cookies we do NOT use

The following types of cookies are **not present** on this site:

- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Hotjar, etc.)
- Advertising or conversion-tracking cookies (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, etc.)
- Social media tracking pixels
- Personalisation or A/B testing cookies

Because all cookies set by this site are strictly necessary, prior consent is not required under GDPR Recital 25 or ePrivacy Directive Article 5(3). We display an informational banner as a transparency measure.

## Managing cookies

You can delete existing cookies or block new ones through your browser settings:

- **Google Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Mozilla Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
- **Microsoft Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

If you block `NEXT_LOCALE` the site will attempt to detect your language from your browser settings on every visit and your preference may not be saved. If you block Cloudflare Turnstile cookies, forms on the site may not work correctly.
