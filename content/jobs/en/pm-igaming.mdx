## What you'll do

As iGaming PM at Airomeda you'll coordinate casino, sportsbook and cashier projects between clients and the engineering team. You'll translate regulatory requirements into sprint stories, own delivery decisions, and manage risk proactively.

Bahis.io platform expansion, TopRateBet payment method integration, Astra Gaming new-jurisdiction launch — you'll be the person who understands both the regulation and the engineering side of every project.

## Is this role for you?

We're looking for someone who knows that iGaming is not just exciting, it's latency-critical and regulation-dense. Someone who can write "we cannot implement this feature due to section 4.2.1 of the licence spec" and then work with the client to find what they *can* have within the 4ms spin response budget.
