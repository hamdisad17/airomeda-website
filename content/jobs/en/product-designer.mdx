## What you'll do

As a Product Designer at Airomeda, you'll turn complex business workflows into intuitive interfaces for client projects in finance, iGaming, and e-commerce. You'll work closely with engineers, tracking how design decisions translate into code quality.

You'll manage design system components in Figma, stay involved in project flow via Linear, and conduct research to simplify user experience on regulation-heavy screens — payment flows, KYC forms, reporting dashboards. Design here is not decoration; it's a tool for making the product actually work.

## Is this role for you?

We're looking for someone who believes design should serve the product team before it serves the end user, is comfortable with complexity, and can speak the language of an engineer. We expect your portfolio to answer "why did you make this decision" as much as "how does it look." If you'd rather ship working products than beautiful screenshots, let's talk.
