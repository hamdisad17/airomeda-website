## About the Project

Ziraat Bank is Turkey's most established and largest state-owned bank. Airomeda developed a solution for one of the most critical needs in the banking sector: personnel performance prediction.

## The Need

Large banking institutions need to evaluate the performance of thousands of employees in a fair, transparent, and data-driven way. Traditional evaluation methods can be subjective, leading to both employee dissatisfaction and management decision errors.

Ziraat Bank wanted to support this process with data science and machine learning technology.

## Solution

The Airomeda team developed a comprehensive performance prediction model using Python and machine learning techniques. The system:

- Identifies patterns from employee historical data and business metrics
- Generates forward-looking performance predictions
- Provides managers with transparent and explainable results
- Fully complies with banking security standards

## Result

The system is actively in use today and provides valuable insights to bank management. This project became Airomeda's strongest reference in AI and data science for the banking sector.

Earning the trust of Turkey's largest institution like Ziraat Bank is the most important proof of our team's technical competence and professionalism.
