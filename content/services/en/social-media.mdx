## Why Airomeda?

The value of social media management lies not just in content production capacity, but in how consistently that content reflects the brand voice and builds genuine engagement with the audience. Rather than filling calendars with posts, we take an approach that understands the dynamics of each channel and updates content strategy based on what performance data reveals.

We have experience managing different content tones across B2B and B2C brands — from thought leadership content on LinkedIn to product storytelling on Instagram. We plan with the channel differences in mind, not despite them.

<Callout title="Our approach">
Strategy before the content calendar, meaning before the metric dashboard.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Declining organic reach and follower trust from inconsistent posting frequency
- Inconsistent content that does not reflect brand voice or visual identity
- Reputation risk from slow or shallow responses to comments and messages
- No data on which content types contribute to conversion
- Channels going dormant due to lack of content production capacity
