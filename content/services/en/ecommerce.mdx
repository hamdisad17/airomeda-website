## Why Airomeda?

E-commerce projects often start with design and frontend, but the real complexity emerges in order management, inventory synchronisation, and the integration layer. We establish every project by clarifying data flows and system boundaries first — so that as scale grows, the infrastructure supports growth rather than constraining it.

Moving to a headless architecture is not just a technology decision; it is an organisational one that lets content teams and development teams work independently. With the right tooling and clear API contracts, we make that transition in planned, measurable steps.

<Callout title="Our approach">
Storefront speed and integration accuracy are treated with the same weight as visual design.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Migrating legacy monolithic e-commerce platforms to headless architecture
- Inventory and order discrepancies across marketplace channels
- Slow load times and usability issues that increase checkout abandonment
- Manual inventory update processes between ERP and WMS systems
- Slow, irrelevant search results across large product catalogues
