## Why Airomeda?

Finance is one of the sectors where the cost of error is highest — a regulatory breach or data leak carries both financial and reputational consequences. Starting from that reality, we begin every finance project by mapping the regulatory framework and grounding our architectural decisions in auditability and security.

We have shipped products in banking, payment processing, and fintech that are in production today. That experience tells us which decisions need to be made early and which technical debt will come due later — helping our clients avoid wasted time and resources.

<Callout title="Our approach">
Regulatory requirements first, delivery speed second — but on a firm schedule.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Migrating monolithic core banking systems to service-based architecture
- Building new digital banking or fintech products from scratch
- Integrating with PSPs, card schemes, and central bank systems
- Automating KYC/AML processes to reduce onboarding time
- Setting up or overhauling regulatory reporting infrastructure
