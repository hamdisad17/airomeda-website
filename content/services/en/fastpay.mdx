## Why FastPay?

FastPay brings together everything a business needs to accept payments in one panel: card and bank-transfer collection, subscriptions and installments, payment links, multi-currency, analytics and corporate security. Instead of building a payment stack from scratch, you start collecting within weeks using a ready, brand-tailored dashboard.

All features ship working in demo mode — try it without entering card details, then go live by connecting your Stripe/iyzico keys.

<Callout title="One panel, all collection">
Card, Apple/Google Pay and bank transfer; subscriptions and installments; multi-currency and coupons — all managed from one place.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Consolidating scattered payment tools into a single panel
- Confirmed management of manual bank-transfer collection alongside cards
- Subscriptions, installments and failed-payment recovery (dunning)
- Real-time tracking of revenue, MRR/ARR, LTV and churn
- Corporate security with role-based access, 2FA and auditable records
- Integration with existing systems via REST API and webhooks

## Highlights

**Customer side:** Live-preview checkout, payment links & QR, customer self-service portal.
**Admin side:** Analytics & BI, risk & dispute management, balance & payouts, team management, AI Copilot and ⌘K quick search.
