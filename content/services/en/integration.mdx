## Why Airomeda?

As an enterprise software ecosystem grows, data consistency across systems becomes one of the most frequently overlooked engineering problems. When order data lives in the ERP, customer data in the CRM, and inventory data in the WMS, the reports feeding business decisions become unreliable.

We treat integration projects not merely as technical wiring tasks, but as a responsibility to keep business processes flowing without interruption. The main reason we prefer event-driven architectures is observability: every message's status is traceable, failed deliveries can be retried, and workflows can scale independently.

<Callout title="Our approach">
Every integration must be observable and reversible — independent of the connected systems.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Inconsistency and error costs caused by data manually copied between systems
- Undocumented or unmaintained point-to-point integration sprawl
- Keeping existing integrations running without interruption during a system migration
- Modernising EDI-based supplier communication
- Delayed detection of integration failures that only surface within business processes
