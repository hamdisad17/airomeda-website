## Why Airomeda?

As a digital marketing budget grows, the inability to read cross-channel data accurately becomes the single largest source of spending inefficiency. When attribution is misconfigured, it becomes unclear which channel is actually driving sales, and budget decisions revert to guesswork.

We always start by building the measurement infrastructure: without correct GA4 and GTM configuration, optimisation is done blind. Having the same team run both technical SEO and ad management means organic and paid channels operate on a strategy that reinforces each other.

<Callout title="Our approach">
Measurement infrastructure first, then optimisation. We rely on data, not assumptions.
</Callout>

## What problems do we solve?

- Not knowing which campaigns in the ad account are actually converting
- Content failing to be properly crawled by search engines due to technical SEO issues
- High cost-per-click combined with low conversion rates
- Missing or incorrect data caused by faulty GA4 or GTM setup
- No capacity to produce content for organic traffic growth
