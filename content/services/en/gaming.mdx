## Why Airomeda?

iGaming is one of the few sectors that must manage strict regulation and high traffic pressure simultaneously. When a platform goes down during a live match, or a payment transaction stalls, the operator faces both revenue loss and license risk.

For backend development we favour Go and Kubernetes-based architectures — a combination proven for low latency and horizontal scalability under iGaming workloads. Keeping game provider APIs isolated behind an adapter layer also means adding new content is possible without slowing down the rest of the platform.

- Migrating monolithic casino platforms to a service-based architecture

- Strengthening fraud and money laundering detection in cashier flows
